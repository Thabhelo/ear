# Ear Product Spec

## Positioning

Ear is for people who need someone real to talk to, listen, or keep them company.

The product does not market loneliness directly. It markets access, conversation, listening, presence, and companionship while preserving dignity on both sides.

Primary message:

> Need someone to talk to? Need someone to listen? Need company while you work? Need a conversation with a real human? Pick a mode and connect.

## Starting Model

Ear starts as:

```text
Customer -> Thabhelo
```

It is not initially:

```text
Customer -> Customer
```

This avoids stranger-to-stranger moderation, harassment, scams, catfishing, safety, and content moderation problems while the product is being proven.

## Modes

### Just Listen

The customer talks. Ear listens. No advice unless requested.

### Conversation

Normal two-way conversation.

### Deep Talk

Life, relationships, dreams, failures, loneliness, and whatever is on the customer's mind.

### Silent Company

Almost no talking. Just another person present.

### Study Buddy

Quiet co-working.

### Game Mode

Bounded game sessions together.

## One-Off Payments

One-off sessions are the acquisition funnel.

| Product | Price | Included |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Quick Call | $2.99 | 3 minutes |
| Standard Call | $6.99 | 25 minutes |
| Long Call | $14.99 | 60 minutes |
| Text Session | $1.99 | Text conversation session |

## Priority Access

Users can add any bid amount. Higher bids move ahead in queue.

Priority score:

```text
priority_score = bid_amount + waiting_time_bonus
```

Waiting time bonus prevents starvation.

The customer experience should show market-based queue information:

```text
Want faster access?
Current Queue:
1. $8
2. $5
3. $3
Place a higher bid?
```

## Grace Extensions

All call durations are minimums.

Calls may continue beyond purchased time when:

- Demand is low.
- Nobody is waiting.
- Energy permits.

Grace extensions are discretionary, free, and not guaranteed.

## Safety And Boundaries

Customers never receive personal contact channels.

Never give:

- Personal phone number.
- Personal email.
- Personal Instagram.
- Personal WhatsApp.

Everything routes through platform accounts.

Ear is not:

- Dating.
- Therapy.
- Medical advice.
- Crisis counseling.
- A substitute for emergency services.

## Call Recording

Every call is recorded. Every participant must consent before joining.

Purpose:

- Safety.
- Quality assurance.
- Dispute resolution.
- Legal protection.

No consent means no call.

Store:

- Recording.
- Timestamp.
- User ID.
- Payment ID.
- Consent record.

## Call Termination

The host can terminate any call immediately with no explanation and no refund required.

Users can leave any call at any time with no explanation required.

Calls automatically disconnect when purchased time ends unless the host grants a grace extension.

## Ban System

### Permanent Ban

For:

- Threats.
- Harassment.
- Sexual misconduct.
- Hate speech.
- Fraud.
- Doxxing.

No appeal.

### Standard Ban

For:

- Repeated disrespect.
- Spam.
- Minor misconduct.
- Boundary violations.

Eligible for review.

### Reinstatement Review

$50.

The fee purchases a manual review. It does not guarantee reinstatement.

If approved, access is restored. If denied, the ban remains.
